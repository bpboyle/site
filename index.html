About Me

My main research areas are comparative politics and political communication. My current work examines EU politics, and political campaigning on twitter. My published work explores the relationship between electoral rules such as compulsory voting and voter registration, and social inequalities across voting, non-elecotral participation, and political attitudes. As well as political communication, looking at political campaigning on twitter, and political representation in UK public broadcasting.

PhD Politics, University of Strathclyde (2017-2022)
