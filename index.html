About Me

My main research interests are political behaviour, political attitudes, and political communication. My published work explores the relationship between electoral rules such as compulsory voting and voter registration, and social inequalities across voting, non-elecotral participation, and political attitudes. As well as political communication, looking at political campaigning on twitter, and political representation in UK public broadcasting.

PhD Politics, University of Strathclyde (2017-2022)
